# Account Recovery

## Locked Account
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts. Customers can self-unlock via the "Forgot Password" flow using their registered email or phone number.

## Compromised Account
If a customer believes their account has been accessed without authorization, treat this as a security incident. Escalate to the account security team immediately, and advise the customer to change their password on all other services where they reused the same credentials.

## Email or Phone Change Requests
Changing the registered email or phone number requires identity verification via OTP to both the old and new contact method. This cannot be done purely through a support chat conversation.
