# Shipping & Damage Policy

## Damaged Item on Arrival
If an item arrives visibly damaged, the customer should report it within 48 hours of delivery with photo evidence. ShopStream will offer a free replacement or a full refund, customer's choice.

## Lost in Transit
If tracking shows no movement for more than 5 days, the order is considered lost in transit and the customer is eligible for a full refund or reshipment.

## Wrong Item Delivered
If the wrong item was delivered, ShopStream arranges free reverse pickup and ships the correct item at no extra cost, or refunds in full if the item is out of stock.

## Packaging Standards
Fragile items are shipped in reinforced packaging. Repeated damage claims on the same account may be flagged for manual review to rule out misuse.
