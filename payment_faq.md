# Payment FAQ

## Payment Failed but Amount Debited
This usually resolves automatically within 24-48 hours as the bank reverses the hold. If it does not reverse within 5 business days, escalate to the payments team with the transaction reference number.

## Duplicate Charge
If a customer is charged twice for the same order, do not attempt to resolve it directly — this must always be escalated to the payments team for manual reconciliation.

## Unrecognized Transaction
If a customer reports a transaction on their account that they do not recognize, treat this as a potential fraud case. Do not attempt to explain it away — escalate immediately to the fraud review team regardless of order value or customer tier.

## EMI and Card Issues
EMI conversion failures or card decline issues at checkout are typically bank-side and the customer should be advised to contact their bank, unless ShopStream's own payment gateway logs show an error on our end.
