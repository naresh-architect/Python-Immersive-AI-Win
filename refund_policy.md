# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window
Customers may request a refund within 10 days of delivery for most product categories. The item must be unused and in its original packaging.

## Refunds Above ₹5000
Any refund request for an order valued above ₹5000 requires manual review by a support supervisor before approval. Agents must not promise or process these refunds automatically.

## Non-Refundable Categories
Innerwear, personal care items, and perishable goods are not eligible for refund once delivered, except in the case of a manufacturing defect.

## Refund Timelines
Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. Store credit refunds are instant.

## Cancelled Orders
Orders cancelled before shipment are refunded in full automatically within 24 hours, with no manual review required.
